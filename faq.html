Frequently Asked Questions

How can I place an order?

You can browse our catalog, add your desired books to the cart, and then proceed to checkout where you can enter your shipping and payment details to complete the order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards, PayPal, and other secure online payment methods.

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping usually takes 5-7 business days within the US. Please refer to our Shipping Info page for more details.

Can I return or exchange an item?

Yes! We have a 30-day return policy on most items. Please review our Returns Policy for full details on how to initiate a return or exchange.

How do I track my order?

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this to track your package online via the carrier’s website.

What if my order arrives damaged?

Please contact our customer service team within 7 days of delivery with photos of the damaged item. We’ll assist you with a replacement or refund.
