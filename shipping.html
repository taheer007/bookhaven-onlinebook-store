Shipping Information

Shipping Options and Delivery Times

We offer the following shipping options to ensure your books reach you promptly and safely:

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days within the US.
  • Expedited Shipping: 2-3 business days within the US.
  • International Shipping: 7-21 business days depending on destination.

Shipping Costs

Shipping fees vary based on your location, order size, and selected shipping method. You can view estimated shipping costs during checkout.

Order Processing

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. You will receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order ships.

Tracking Your Order

Use the tracking number in your shipping confirmation email to monitor your shipment status online.

Lost or Damaged Items

If your order is lost or arrives damaged, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of delivery for assistance.
